Frequently Asked Questions

How do I request funding for my companies project?

+ -

To request for funding, simply go to the "Submit Application" button located at the top right hand of the device you are using. You will be redirected to to an application form where youst must fill all fields that are highlighted with a * next to the field. Upon finishing the form simply hit the submit button and our services will recieve the request, and you will hear from us via the email that you used to create your account with. - Uni from Customer Support


How do I create an account?

+ -

To create an account simply click on the "Login" butoon that is located at the top right of the screen. There you will be prompted to "Log in" with an account, however you want to click on the link titled Sign Up. Here you will be prompted to create an account using an username, email and password. From the there you can go to the log in screen and simply log in with the account you just created. - Dengekiko from Customer Support


How do I review a previously submitted application?

+ -

To view a previously submitted application, simply click on the "View Applciation" button located at the top right of the device your using. This will redirected you to the submission history section which will show all the applications that you have previously submitted. These submissions will either be Green if the submission has been viewed and feedback has been sent via email, Orange if the submission has been submitted but feedback has not been given, Blue if the submission has been submitted but has not been reviewed by a member of our staff, and lastly Red if an error has occured when submitting an application. (In the case of an error please call 080203923 or email us at Lastion@email.com to request assistence from one of our internal staff members) - Uni from Customer Support
